Ontario has a thriving and democratic society built by the hard work of generations of immigrants. Toronto, its capital, is a great meeting place, receives people from all over the world who with their cultural contribution enrich the city, creating an incredible fusion of traditions, passions and different ways of seeing life.

Toronto consists of six areas: Downtown (Downtown), North York, East York, York, Scarborough and Etobicoke. In any of them you will find a variety of ethnic neighbourhoods, such as Chinatown, Little Italy, Little Portugal, Greek Quarter, Little India and Little Poland. Without a doubt, it is one of the most dynamic, energetic and creative cities in the world.

Like any cosmopolitan city, Toronto offers endless options for living. Its particular glass buildings offer modern common areas for its residents such as gym, indoor pool and party rooms. You can also find beautiful homes and townhouses with a unique and spectacular style. And if family atmosphere is concerned, the suburbs on the outskirts of the city are an excellent choice for those who prefer a more quiet and comfortable life.
